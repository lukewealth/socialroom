Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about The Social Boardroom, membership, benefits, and how to get involved.

What is The Social Boardroom?

The Social Boardroom is a community where professionals collaborate, share ideas, and support each other in finding purpose and creating meaningful solutions.

Who can join The Social Boardroom?

Membership is open to professionals, entrepreneurs, and individuals eager to grow, network, learn, and contribute to insightful discussions.

How do I join the community?

Follow the invitation link provided on our social media pages, click it, and complete the sign-up process.

Is there a membership fee?

Yes, there is a one-time yearly membership fee of $30, payable upon entry.

What are the benefits of being a member?

Members gain access to exclusive workshops, networking events, mentorship programs, resources, and a supportive community of like-minded professionals.

How often does the community meet?

We host regular meetings, workshops, and networking events. Check our announcements channel for upcoming dates.

Can I suggest topics for discussions or events?

Yes! We encourage members to share ideas. You can submit suggestions in the designated channel or contact a moderator.

Are there mentorship opportunities?

Yes, we have mentorship programs where experienced members guide those seeking professional or business support.

Can I promote my business or services?

Self-promotion is allowed only in designated channels. Unsolicited promotions in other channels are not permitted.

Can I leave the community and rejoin later?

Yes, you can leave anytime. If you wish to rejoin later, a $30 re-entry fee will apply.

Are there specific channels I should know about?

Yes, we have channels for introductions, announcements, general discussions, resources, and events. Check the channel list for details.
